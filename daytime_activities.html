    Hiking

    Want to enjoy the mountain breeze and the morning fresh air? Join our morning hiking activity! Here you can hike safely with our professional guide that will lead you through the safest path to the peak of the mountain. Throughout the hike, you can enjoy the forest’s ambient noises and nature’s ambience.

    Once you reach the peak, you will be able to see the wonderful sunrise and gaze at the amazing view. You are also given 15 to 20 minutes to enjoy the atmosphere and the view at the top of the mountain. While at the peak, you can opt to have a quick snack that will be provided by our staff. You can choose between our specially prepared tuna sandwiches or egg mayo sandwiches that are ideal for a quick snack.

    ATV

    Tap on the image to expand it!

    Note: Please be aware that only children ages 16 and above are allowed to participate in this activity due to safety concerns. Thank you for your understanding.

    Don’t feel like hiking? Want to enjoy a thrilling experience? Join our Off-Road Madness ATV ride! Sounds crazy right? Don’t worry it is completely safe as there will be a designated track for you to drive through that provides you with the most fun and exciting experience while also prioritizing your safety. Furthermore, we have set safety rules and our staff will always ensure that you have passed all our safety checks before allowing you to start your ATV ride adventure. Helmets, riding jackets and pants are provided to ensure that you are well-protected and prepared for the muddy conditions!

    Be sure to come prepared with clothes that you can move around in because, trust me, you’ll need that extra mobility when riding the ATV. Also, you are reminded to bring a fresh pair of clothes to change into because things can get really sweaty after the atv ride. If you just so happen to forget bringing a change of clothes, no worries, as you can opt to rent clothes instead from our booth. The clothes will be automatically collected by our staff at your rooms the next day once you are done using it.
